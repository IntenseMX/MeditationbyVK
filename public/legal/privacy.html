Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2024

Introduction

Meditation by VK ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Anonymous user ID (Firebase Authentication)
  • Premium subscription status
  • Meditation progress and favorites

Usage Data

  • App interactions and meditation play history
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and performance data

Payment Information

  • Handled entirely by Apple/Google
  • We receive only subscription status confirmation
  • We do NOT store credit card or payment details

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the App
  • Sync meditation progress across devices
  • Manage premium subscriptions
  • Send important service updates (not marketing)

Data Storage

  • Data stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Located in United States
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share only with:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud (infrastructure provider)
  • Apple/Google (payment processing only)

Your Rights

  • Access your data: Contact us for export
  • Delete your data: Delete account in app settings
  • Opt-out: Stop using the App

Children's Privacy

App not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account exists
  • Deleted accounts: Data purged within 30 days
  • Legal requirements may require longer retention

International Users

Data may be transferred to and stored in the United States.

Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Changes posted with new "Last Updated" date.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@meditationbyvk.com

GDPR/CCPA Rights

EU/California residents have additional rights. Contact us for details.
